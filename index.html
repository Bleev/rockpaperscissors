How to Play Rock, Paper Scissors:

Rock, Paper, Scissors is very simple. It's a game between two sides where each side has 3 choices. Both can choose either Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Each choice beats another but can also be beat by the other. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock!
